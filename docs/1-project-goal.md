# Project Goal — Global Job Finder Bot

## What I'm Trying to Achieve
I want a personal automation tool that finds relevant job openings for me from around the world, filters them down to what actually matters to me, and sends me a clean, ranked list — without me having to manually browse LinkedIn/Indeed every day.

## My Situation
- I'm based in **Pakistan**.
- I'm open to **local Pakistan jobs** (no relocation needed there).
- My main focus is international roles in:
  - **Europe**
  - **USA**
  - **Australia**
  - **Canada**
  - **New Zealand**
- For these international roles, I only want ones that offer **visa sponsorship / relocation assistance** — I can't apply to roles that expect me to already have a work permit or be locally based.

## What the Bot Should Do
1. Continuously check multiple job sources (not just one board) for new listings.
2. Only show me jobs posted **recently** — last 24 hours up to the last 7 days (I don't want stale listings).
3. Automatically separate:
   - Pakistan jobs → shown as-is, no relocation filter.
   - International jobs → shown **only if** they mention visa sponsorship or relocation support.
4. Compare each job against my resume/skills and rank the best matches at the top, so I'm not wasting time on irrelevant postings.
5. Send me a **digest** (Telegram message) automatically, on a schedule, with no manual step required to "check" for jobs.
6. (Later upgrade) Help me apply faster by pre-filling application forms — but I will always personally review and click submit myself.

## What This Is NOT
- It is **not** a LinkedIn/Indeed scraper or bot that logs into my account — I don't want to risk getting my account banned.
- It is **not** a fully automatic mass-apply system — I want to stay in control of what I actually submit.
- It is **not** meant to be a complex enterprise system — just a solo personal tool, low-cost or free to run.

## Success Looks Like
- I open Telegram and see a short, ranked list of genuinely new, genuinely relevant, genuinely visa-friendly (or Pakistan-based) jobs — without opening a browser or searching manually.
- I never see the same job twice.
- I spend my time applying to good matches, not searching for them.
